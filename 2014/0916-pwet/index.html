Objectives of the task force

Foster a positive, supportive and safe work environment within the broad W3C Community, by developing:

  • W3C community principles
  • A procedural framework

Deliverables

  • W3C Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct
  • Procedures (a section in PWE public homepage)
  • DRAFT: W3C Conference Code of Conduct

A code?

  • Needed
  • Adds value
  • Tool for cultural change
  • Complements Process and other policies

W3C Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct

The W3C Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct defines acceptable behavior and promotes high standards of professional practice, e.g.:

  • Respect
  • No harassment
  • No discrimination

+ comprehensive glossary

Procedures

  • Legal first
  • Timely
  • W3C Ombuds & CEO
  • No requirement to contact a particular Ombuds

W3C Ombuds

  • Trusted third party
  • Appointed by Hosts and W3C Management
  • Familiar with PWE and W3C culture
  • Handle informal resolution; formal complaints

Ombuds will operate by procedures appropriate to their sites and jurisdictions.
